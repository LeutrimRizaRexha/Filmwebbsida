Snowfall

The famous crime drama TV show first aired it's episode in 2017. Its creators Eric Amadio, John Singleton and Dave Andron have since launched 6 seasons.

The series takes us back to the 80's and the crackcocain epidemic which shook the whole of America. We get to see how a young and smart boy grows up and builds an empire. On the way to the top many relationships get created and many others break. Eventually it ends in a way to show the thruth about the illiegal buisness that many fall in too.
